About Us

Welcome to Fashion Gallery General Trading L.L.C! Your go-to destination for all things fashion. Established in January 2021, we specialize in handling an exquisite collection of fashion accessories including watches, bags, wallets, sunglasses, electronics, toys and more. As a retailer sourcing directly from trusted distributors such as Al Futtaim Company, Ahmed Seddiqi and Sons, and Rivoli in the UAE, as well as other countries, we ensure that our customers have access to the latest trends and top-notch quality. With a focus on watches, we offer both wholesale and retail options for renowned fashion brands like Emporio Armani, Fossil, Guess, Seiko, Casio, G-Shock, Diesel, Anne Klein, Puma, Ferrari and many more. Join us on our fashion journey and discover the perfect piece that reflects your style effortlessly.
